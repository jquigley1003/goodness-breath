The Goodness Breath Project

Light Up the Grid with Goodness

The Goodness Breath came to me as a directive and ongoing spiritual practice for all of us to participate in at this time of upheaval and transformation. It is a way of staying in powerful alignment with who we really are as human beings and what we are made of - goodness.

In these challenging times of witnessing our government enact war on our environment (wildlife, air and water, earth, national parks), on immigrants (e.g., separating parents from children), on our human rights and the very values that our democracy was founded on, it is so easy to “go low” — to match the ugliness with our own rage, disgust, fear and hatred.

However, in order to turn things around, we are called to do the very opposite.  We need to take the high road, as in Michelle Obama’s declaration, “When they go low, we go high.”  And since virtually every day there is another assault on our democratic values, we must consistently practice going “high.”

The formula is simple for The Goodness Breath.

Tap on the 3 steps:



1. Breathe In 2. Take a Moment 3. Breathe Out

Breathe in and imagine filling ourselves up by drawing from the well of our own inherent goodness.

Take a moment to consciously experience the beneficent power of this goodness.

Breathe out, intentionally channeling this goodness into our country and our world as a force of light.

Let's explore the depths of each step:

1. Breathe in and fill up with the goodness that dwells at the core of our being. In order to do this, we must remember that goodness lives within us. A lot of people don’t have faith in their own goodness and often feel “not good enough” or “unworthy” in some way. These negative stories about ourselves must be seen for what they are — conditioning and narratives we have made up to explain negative experiences in our lives, usually in our younger years. The call is to relinquish any doubt about the fact of our own inherent goodness.

2. Intentionally drink of this goodness and pause long enough to ideally feel the richness of goodness in your body. But whether we feel it or not, the goodness is there. There are various approaches to being aware of the goodness: Breathe into your heart. Acknowledge your essence. Pause and experience your essence as goodness. Some people describe this process as reaching deep inside of themselves, beyond their stories and personalities, into the spiritual well of our oneness. Others tell me that they intentionally tune into a sense of divinity or light within themselves that they believe is in all of us. Still others focus on the giving and receiving of kindness as a way to viscerally fill themselves up with goodness. Of course, feel free to experiment with various ways of tuning into goodness, and discover whatever works best for you. Bottom line, what is most important is that you find your way to access the goodness within you and you pause long enough to savor, in some manner, this essential goodness. Even one conscious moment can be transformative.

3. Intentionally channel this goodness out to others, into our country and our world as a force of light. With your exhalation, you propel this goodness from your heart and center, out into the world. You can use sound and body movement if this feels empowering for you. Or you can just use the power of visualization and intention to propel this Goodness out into the world as a force for global healing.

Light Up the Grid with Goodness

“I am one person remembering... and now I am two and now ten and now twenty and now the twenty becomes twenty million as we light up our Earth’s grid with goodness as a global prayer-in-action”

This is what it is going to take — all over the world. We wake up to the force of our goodness and join together in a global movement.

Imagine millions of humans, all across the world, participating in this focused spiritual practice — people of all races and languages, rich and poor, young and old — all breathing in goodness, breathing out goodness as a fierce force for transformation.

So, how does one person become millions of people committed to Lighting Up the Grid with Goodness?

Let's make it happen.

Make your commitment by placing your name and location on www.goodnessbreath.com

* Make it real by making it a habit to practice the goodness breath constantly throughout the day so that it becomes a part of your daily life focus.

* Breathe in Goodness and Breathe out Goodness consciously every day at least for five minutes.

* Invite everyone you know to join forces with the Goodness Breath Project. Bring it into your community, your family, your circle of friends, your school, your place of worship. Make it your own to share with as many people as you can and spread the word.

* Practice it for yourself, for all of us humans, and for all beings who share Mother Earth as our home.

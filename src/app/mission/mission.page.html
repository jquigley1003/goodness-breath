Our Mission

The Vision and Mission:

The Mission is to enroll as many human beings possible across our planet to participate in the intentional practice of The Goodness Breath. We rise up in opposition to the massive expressions of fear, greed and division.

As we make this practice a part of our daily lives, we implement our conscious intention to turn things around. This conscious inner posture that anchors us into our goodness can be foundational for all the other actions we take.

We powerfully align with our goodness and intentionally direct it out into the world as a way to propel the best of what makes us human.

In these days when the shadow side of our humanity is so prevalent and seemingly out of control, The Goodness Breath Project is dedicated to utilizing our inherent, essential goodness as a force for positive change and transformation at this most critical time in our evolution.

As more and more of us join together in this mission to stand firm in our goodness and to radiate it out all over the world, the power of our Goodness gains strength and gathers the momentum necessary for us to turn things around. We believe our inherent, essential Goodness has the power to unite and awaken us in powerful ways so that we can overcome and reverse the racism, tyranny and destructiveness that is currently showing itself around the world. From the vibration of affirming our goodness, the actions we take to make a difference are enhanced with this high frequency.

This shift in consciousness calls upon us to anchor into the belief that the light is more powerful than the darkness.

We who join in conscious goodness are an army of warriors of radical awareness and action.

“Warriors” because we are taking it on as a mission to “take the high road,” no matter what human ugliness we may encounter.

“Radical awareness” because it is a refusal to identify with what is before us and surrounding us.

“Action” because we hold to goodness consciously and we refuse to allow ourselves to be diminished.

And we bring this goodness into any and all opportunities to show up with kindness, generosity and service.

It is going to take a critical mass of us to become conscious and harness the power of love as stronger than anything else.

By joining together in this unified focus on spreading goodness all around the globe, we become a force that awakens our sense of unity and respect for the equality of all beings and for our earth.

The calling is for each and every one of us to consciously practice this Goodness Breath throughout the day, as often as we can remember to. We simply breathe in and fill up on goodness, savor it, and then exhale this goodness out into the world with a positive and fierce intention.

How do we stay tuned into the goodness when we become so stirred up with anger, horror and aversion?

We must give permission to ourselves to feel what we feel. We must create a time and place to feel these feelings and then learn to shift the focus back to our core selves.

The first step, even before utilizing the various tools to transform the negativity, is to anchor into the conviction of why it is so important to make the shift back to our goodness.

In the face of darkness, the Fierce Voice of Goodness declares:

I will not identify with you. I will not match you in darkness.

I will not lower my vibration to match yours.

I will not let you fool me into believing that human beings are this bad.

I will not allow myself to hate you as much as you hate others.

I will not give up my power by allowing this kind of hate to take me over.

I will courageously stare you in your face -- a human face just like mine.

I will witness you as you do actions that hurt others and our earth.

In response, I may cry and scream and rage and fall to my knees and pound the earth.

But I refuse to identify with you.

I will not join you in the ugliness.

No! I will not forget who I am

I stand steady and strong.

 

I am goodness, more powerful than the shadows that you cast.

I am endless acts of kindness and service.

I am goodness.

This goodness is light.

Your darkness can not put my light out.

In the face of you, I will not forget who I am.

You are vapor. You will come and go. You will not rule me.

Goodness prevails and is ultimately unconquerable.

Now, once you anchor into this conviction, actively work with yourself to make the shift and clear the way to experience inherent Goodness.

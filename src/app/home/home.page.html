The Goodness Breath Project

Light Up the Grid with Goodness

The Goodness Breath is an ongoing spiritual practice for all of us to participate in at this time of upheaval andtransformation. It is a way of staying in powerful alignment with who we really are as human beings and what we are made of - goodness. Show More

In these challenging times of witnessing our government enact war on our environment (wildlife, air and water, earth, national parks), on immigrants (e.g., separating parents from children), on our human rights and the very values that our democracy was founded on, it is so easy to “go low” — to match the ugliness with our own rage, disgust, fear and hatred.

However, in order to turn things around, we are called to do the very opposite.  We need to take the high road, as in Michelle Obama’s declaration, “When they go low, we go high.”  And since virtually every day there is another assault on our democratic values, we must consistently practice going “high.”

Light Up the Grid with Goodness

“I am one person remembering... and now I am two and now ten and now twenty and now the twenty becomes twenty million as we light up our Earth’s grid with goodness as a global prayer-in-action”

This is what it is going to take — all over the world. We wake up to the force of our goodness and join together in a global movement.

Imagine millions of humans, all across the world, participating in this focused spiritual practice — people of all races and languages, rich and poor, young and old — all breathing in goodness, breathing out goodness as a fierce force for transformation.

So, how does one person become millions of people committed to Lighting Up the Grid with Goodness?
